Announcement Welcome

Welcome to My Blog

Welcome! I'm excited to launch this blog as a space to share my thoughts on topics I'm passionate about: UX design, tech ethics, instructional design, and the intersection of technology and human wellbeing.

Why I'm Starting This Blog

Throughout my journey from digital media design to pursuing a Master of Social Work, I've found myself at a unique intersection of disciplines. My work with Jonathan Haidt on The Anxious Generation, my experience building learning systems for human service workers, and my clinical training have all given me perspectives I want to explore and share.

This blog will be a place to think out loud about:

  • UX Design - User research methods, accessibility, and designing with intention
  • Tech Ethics - How technology shapes our lives and wellbeing
  • Instructional Design - Building effective learning experiences
  • The Human Side of Tech - Bringing clinical insights to design work

What to Expect

I plan to write about case studies from my work, reflections on readings and research, and practical insights that might be useful for others working in these spaces. My goal is to bridge the gap between academic research and practical application.
