Overview

WeDo is a mobile app designed to make wedding planning accessible and affordable for everyone. This project was completed as part of the Google UX Design Certificate program, demonstrating end-to-end UX design skills from initial research through final prototyping.

The Challenge

Wedding planning can be overwhelming and expensive. Many couples struggle to find affordable options and organize all the details of their special day. WeDo addresses this challenge by providing a user-friendly platform that helps couples plan their dream wedding without breaking the bank.

Key Takeaways

This project reinforced the importance of user-centered design principles and iterative prototyping. Through user research, wireframing, and usability testing, the design evolved to better meet the needs of couples planning their weddings on a budget.
